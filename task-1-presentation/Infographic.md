## 1. Task 1 – Infographic
**Focus:** Visual Communication & Information Design
* **Process:** For this task, I selected a design tool (e.g., Canva/Adobe Express) to transform a complex topic into a visually digestible format. The goal was to balance aesthetic quality with comprehensive topic coverage.
* **Reflection:** I learned that effective design isn't just about "looking good"—it’s about information hierarchy. Ensuring that the most critical data points stood out while maintaining a clean layout was the primary challenge and success of this task.
