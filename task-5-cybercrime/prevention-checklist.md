# Stay Safe Online Prevention Checklist -

1. **The Golden Rule of UPI:** Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money. A PIN is only required for sending or authorizing a debit, never for a credit.
2. **Verify the Source:** Before scanning any QR code or clicking a payment link, verify the sender's VPA (Virtual Payment Address) to ensure it is a legitimate merchant.
3. **Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** Activate 2FA on all academic (VIT email), social, and financial accounts to provide an extra layer of security beyond passwords.
4. **Official Reporting Channels:** Immediately report any cyber fraud on the **National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in)** or call the **Helpline 1930**.
5. **Secure Your Devices:** Use biometric locks or strong passphrases for your banking apps and avoid using public Wi-Fi for any financial transactions.
6. **Fact-Check Information:** Never act on "urgent" messages claiming your bank account or SIM card will be blocked. Contact your bank directly through official channels.
7. **Audit App Permissions:** Regularly check which apps have access to your SMS and Contacts to prevent data harvesting or "SMS Forwarding" scams.
8. **Stay Updated:** Keep your phone's operating system and all financial apps updated to ensure you have the latest security patches against vulnerabilities.
